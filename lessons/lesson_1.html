Classes and Lessons

This page contains classes and lessons created by Don't-mestic Violence for the purpose of educating the public on the reality of domestic violence, how to effectively deal with it, and the best ways to help victims.

Lesson 1 - What is domestic violence

This lesson will guide you to understand what constitutes domestic violence and the harm that it can cause.

Lesson 2 - Identifying signs of domestic violence

This lesson will teach you the signs of a violent relationship or domestic violence.

Lesson 3 - Leaving a violent relationship

This lesson will teach you how to exit a violent relationship, and prepare you with resources in the event that it is necessary.

Lesson 4 - Helping another person in a violent relationship

Lesson 4 will show you what resources are available to help others who are in a violent relationship or experiencing domestic violence.
